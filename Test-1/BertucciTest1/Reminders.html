Pemberly Furniture and Interiors Reminder List

  1. Ask Yourself Questions about places you felt especially comfortable
    1. How did those places make you feel?
    2. What did you like about them?
    3. What was the color scheme?
    4. Was the room cluttered or spare?
    5. Was the furniture made of wood or metal, or was it upholstered?
    6. Would you describe the room as formal or casual?
  2. Pick a Style
    1. Formal
    2. Traditional
    3. Eclectic
    4. Contemporary
  3. Consider making your purchases in the following order:
    1. Large items such as sofas, beds, and dining tables
    2. Lamps or other forms of lighting
    3. End tables and other accent pieces
    4. Rugs or other floor coverings
